For Global Game Jam 2026, our team of three created *Ticket Tails*, a game about three raccoons in a trench coat trying to sneak into a movie theatre. The game is a 2D sidescroller where up to three players can control raccoons as they try to avoid security guards and solve puzzles to scrounge up enough money to buy tickets to the theatre. 
<br/><br/>
*Ticket Tails* features a variety of levels, each with their own unique challenges and obstacles, as well as a simple AI for the security guards. The game is no longer in development, however it is available to play through the **[Global Game Jam website](https://globalgamejam.org/games/2026/ticket-tails-4)**. It was a great learning experience and I am excited to be working on more games in the future!
